Changed characters

Lilex — curvy parentheses

Moxy borrows Lilex's curvier ( ) (behind lilx; baked into the static fonts). Recursive's parentheses are straighter.

Lilex — added arrows (absent in Recursive)

These 12 single-character arrows are grafted from Lilex and always-on in Moxy. Recursive has no glyph for any of them, so the Recursive column shows —.

Lilex — contextual ligatures

Behind lilx (default-on in the static build). |> / <| join the bar to the arrowhead; runs of ---+ connect into one stroke; a backslash thins when it's an escape (\n, \\ — the 1st of a pair thins, the 2nd stays normal).

Directly drawn — SF Mono–inspired

Drawn from scratch (OFL-clean) and always-on in both VF and static. These override Recursive's own ✓ • / \ % $ @ & and the numerals 2 4 5 7. All are two-master — they thicken with weight. The SF row shows SF Mono (system font) for reference — the target aesthetic.
